Transporting these bins is very expensive so he tries to keep them at any given location for as long as possible, emptying them once a week. He's willing to keep a bin at a given location as long as it is full at the end of each week.</p>

<p>Hex has very reliable estimates of the amount of recyclable materials (in cubic meters) that he can expect each week at each location.  Given this information he would like to know when to install the recycling bin of an appropriate size to maximize the amount of material recycled. He will keep the bin at that location up to (but not including) the week when they don't expect it to be filled to capacity.</p>
