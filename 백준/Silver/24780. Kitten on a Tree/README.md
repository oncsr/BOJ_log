# [24780. Kitten on a Tree](https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/24780)

## 문제
Ouch! A kitten got stuck on a tree.  Fortunately, the tree's branches are numbered. Given a description of a tree and the position of the kitten, can you write a program to help the kitten down?

## 예제 입력 1
```
14
25 24
4 3 1 2
13 9 4 11
10 20 8 7
32 10 21
23 13 19 32 22
19 12 5 14 17 30
14 6 15 16
30 18 31 29
24 23 26
26 27 28
-1
```

## 예제 출력 1
```
14 19 23 24 25
```
